About Me

My name is Ruth Stevens and I currently am located in Cincinnati, Ohio. In 2020 I graduated with my bachelor's degree in visual arts from the University of Toledo. I initially thought that that was what I wanted, but learned that some things are better kept for yourself to enjoy. I also love to play the sims on my computer and spend time with my fur-baby, M'baku. He is learning just as much as I am, as I am constantly talking to him while I work on projects.

I have met many people recently who do coding and software engineering and they thought I might like it, so here I am, learning all that I can and putting my work on display. Just for you! As I create new projects I will put them here on this site and make them easy to access by linking the image of the homepage to the projects themselves.
